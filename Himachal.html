HIMACHAL PARDESH

Himachal Pradesh is popularly known as the Devbhumi "Land of the Gods", Himachal Pradesh is a state in Northern India, a beautiful hill state in India, nestles in north-west region of western Himalayas. Himachal is situated on the bank of River Beas, Himachal boasts of much more than just the serene beauty. The Dalai Lama resides in this Himalayan Mountain state, home to many Buddhist & Hindu temples.

The entire State is punctuated with stone as well as wood temples. The shadowy valleys, rugged crags, glaciers and gigantic pines and roaring rivers and exquisite flora and fauna compose the symphony that is for ever Himachal.
